My Story

My name is Sir. Robert of the Crazy's III. However you can call me Crazy Bob. I inherited this pumpkin patch from my father who got it from his father. I'm a third generation farmer of pumpkins. My goal of this business is to give the joy of carving pumpkins to everyone I can!
